# Rough Runway Design Questionnaire

## 1. Brand Identity & Theme Direction

### 1.1 Overall Aesthetic Preferences
- What emotions should the design evoke? (e.g., professional, trustworthy, innovative, playful)
- Do you prefer minimalist or rich/decorative design?
- Any specific industries or brands that inspire you visually?

### 1.2 Theme Direction
Based on our initial conversation, here are three potential directions:

**Option A: Swiss Minimalist**
- Clean, precise lines
- Mountain runway aesthetic
- Red and white color scheme
- Helvetica/neutral typography
- Focus on clarity and precision

**Option B: Retro Aviation**
- 60s-80s airplane instrumentation vibe
- Vintage flight仪表 inspiration
- Warm metallic tones
- Rounded corners and retro elements
- "Build the plane as you fly" concept

**Option C: Modern Crypto-Native**
- Contemporary tech aesthetic
- Digital/financial data visualization focus
- Bold, contrasting colors
- Sharp, modern typography
- Futuristic elements

**Questions:**
1. Which direction resonates most with you?
2. What elements from each would you like to incorporate?
3. Are there any directions you want to avoid completely?

### 1.3 Brand Personality
1. If Rough Runway were a person, how would you describe them?
2. What are 3-5 adjectives that should describe the brand?
3. How should it feel different from typical financial software?

- Hard Runway (stable assets only)
