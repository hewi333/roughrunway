# Rough Runway Theme Concepts

> **Status: Historical / exploration.**
> Decision locked on a **Swiss Aviation + Retro Aviation hybrid** ("Retro Swiss Aviation"), April 22, 2026. The active spec is [`DESIGN-IMPLEMENTATION.md`](./DESIGN-IMPLEMENTATION.md). Concepts 1 and 2 below fed into the final direction; Concept 3 (Modern Crypto-Native) was discarded.

## Concept 1: Swiss Minimalist Aviation

### Visual Inspiration
- Swiss design principles: clean, precise, functional
- Mountain runway aesthetics
- Minimalist approach with maximum clarity
- Red and white color scheme (Swiss flag influence)

### Key Elements
- Clean typography (Helvetica or similar)
- Ample whitespace
- Precise geometric elements
- Focus on essential information
- Subtle aviation-inspired details

### Color Palette Direction
- Primary: Clean red (#FF0000) or deep burgundy
- Secondary: Pure white and various grays
- Accents: Sky blue for extended runway
- Background: Crisp white/light gray

### Typography
- Helvetica Neue or similar Swiss typeface
- Clear hierarchy with size and weight
- Minimal font variations
- High readability for financial data

### Emotional Tone
- Professional, trustworthy
- Precise, reliable
- Clean, uncluttered
- Focused on essential information

## Concept 2: Retro Aviation (60s-80s)

### Visual Inspiration
- Vintage aircraft instrumentation
- Classic flight仪表 design
- Warm metallic tones
- Rounded corners and retro elements
- "Build the plane as you fly" philosophy

### Key Elements
- Warm metallic colors (brass, copper, steel)
- Rounded corner rectangles
- Instrument-like gauges and indicators
- Retro typography
- Analog-inspired data visualization

### Color Palette Direction
- Primary: Warm metallic gold/brass
- Secondary: Deep navy blue or forest green
- Accents: Warm orange or amber
- Background: Cream or light taupe

### Typography
- Retro sans-serif or modified serif
- Slight rounded corners on letters
- Vintage-inspired numerals
- Monospace for data display

### Emotional Tone
- Nostalgic, trustworthy
- Hands-on, craft-focused
- Adventure and exploration
- Personal piloting experience

## Concept 3: Modern Crypto-Native

### Visual Inspiration
- Contemporary tech aesthetic
- Digital finance interfaces
- Bold, high-contrast elements
- Data visualization focus
- Futuristic but accessible

### Key Elements
- Bold geometric shapes
- High-contrast color combinations
- Modern sans-serif typography
- Digital-inspired data displays
- Clean, sharp lines

### Color Palette Direction
- Primary: Bold electric blue or purple
- Secondary: Deep dark background
- Accents: Vibrant contrasting colors
- Data visualization: Specific colors for asset types

### Typography
- Modern geometric sans-serif
- Clear hierarchy with size and weight
- Monospace for data elements
- High contrast for readability

### Emotional Tone
- Innovative, cutting-edge
- Tech-savvy, modern
- Data-driven and precise
- Forward-thinking
